Employment narrative

As a data professional, I have a passion for working with data and using it to drive business success. I honed my skills in data cleaning and preparation using Python, utilizing libraries such as Pandas and NumPy to handle missing values, outliers, and inconsistent data.

I also have a talent for creating and maintaining performance dashboards using Tableau. Through these dashboards, I am able to evaluate the performance of each cluster and branch and effectively communicate my findings to stakeholders through monthly presentations.

In addition to my technical skills, I have experience managing people and resources. I was responsible for recruiting L1 & L2 employees and training them to connect with clients and pursue business, leading to the formation of strong, sustained customer relationships.

One of my proudest achievements was enhancing the performance of a whole cluster from 84% to 98% in just four months. I accomplished this by establishing robust controls on overtime and closely monitoring any deviations through micro-management.

I am also a natural salesperson, consistently exceeding monthly loan disbursal targets and product sales. I trained my staff on upselling techniques for blue lemon products, resulting in even greater success. Despite the challenges posed by the COVID pandemic, I was able to effectively handle a portfolio worth Rs. 33 crores across 7 branches.

Through my hard work, dedication, and expertise, I have made a positive impact on the businesses I have worked for. I am truly a master of my craft and am eager to share my skills and experience with others.
